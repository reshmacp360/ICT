Kottayam


Kottayam is a city in the Indian state of Kerala. Kottayam literally means the interior of a fort "Kotta" meaning fort and "Akam" meaning inside. Flanked by the Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west, Kottayam is a place that is known for extraordinary qualities


Must visit places :
Illikkal Kallu
Kumarakam
